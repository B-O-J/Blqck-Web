About the Author

This collection, BlQCK: A Mirror in the Dark, is not just poetry, it is reflection. Each verse is a step into solitude, faith, and awakening; capturing the whispers of consciousness that often slip through silence.

Through shadow and light, these words attempt to illuminate the spaces between knowing and not knowing, between the soul and the self.

May you find fragments of yourself in these verses, and may the journey through them bring you closer to your own reflection in the dark.
