We speak different languages and if there is a level of consciousness, meeting an individual that speaks the same language is inevitable. Ignorance, they say is bliss, but is it really; is there curiousity or outright ignorance? The mind is always curious and there is no limit to the extent it can go, it leads to Insanity; rigidity is the death of the mind. Which is preferable, death or insanity?

Weakness sometimes is certain, how strong then will an individual be? It is a selfish world so ultimately self care is of utmost importance.

The journey through hell is long, dark and lonely, very few survive to see the other side. On the other side is a life of isolation and solitude, what better life is there to live if not a life of solace? If an individual is ignorant of darkness, can he appreciate true happiness? Meeting someone that speaks the same language guarantees nothing, because two entities are distinctly different. However this is guaranteed; Absence of value guarantees loneliness, neglect and suffering.

Who then is reliable? Look around and what is inevitable is nature and its magnificence. Who designed it all? God is perfect but in that perfection, there is a flaw; humans are made in His image and if an individual wants to see God, he should look within, but humans are capable of evil as the Holy Book says “the heart of man is wicked….” Life can be cruel, evil and vile; that could be God’s wrath punishing an individual for disobedience, be strong enough, soon He will provide respite; that is God’s perfection.

His wrath is the journey through hell; is God evil?

To err is human, to forgive is divine. An individual’s disobedience should be forgiven if he asks for forgiveness, after-all he is human; how disobedient then can an individual be to incur God’s wrath and a journey through hell? Pray for strength, pray for courage, pray for guidance; because the journey in hell is certain.
