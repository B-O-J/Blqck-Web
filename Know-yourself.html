Behave like them but do not become them; what a life, what a burden! It is both demanding and exhausting remaining a self and not losing that self. Seem too different, you risk being hated and maligned. Conform, then you risk being one of them with no voice or reason. We all wear masks; present a person, else they impose a persona that’s not you.

Far too many live in an illusion wearing a mask trying to fit in, wandering too far away from reality, aimlessly fantasizing about an idealistic world. Losing the sense of self living in delusion, the truth seems alien. The more delusional, the longer the suffering.

Know yourself.
