"I am not made of light, I am the flicker before the flame."

There is a hunger in silence. The kind that gnaws at truth. I do not write to be heard, I write because I echo.

Pages don’t bleed, but sometimes they ache.

"Some dreams don’t die; they just wait in dark corners."

I remember the weight of things unsaid more than the weight of memory. Perhaps the heart is just a grave for untold poems.
