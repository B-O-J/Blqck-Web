How do you silence the voices in your head; drugs, alcohol, women or death?

In your weakness, how strong will you be - maybe all you need is awareness because even the strongest man has a weakness.

It is dangerous to be weak, predators are ruthless and they bite ferociously but awareness in moments of weakness saves you from the traps laid out - predators are sharp and always on the prowl.

Indulge yourself and your awareness will make you comfortable enough to recharge, you will realize all they want is to be heard and acknowledged.

Then they are silent, only to begin again until the silence is permanent.

You are the 'voices' in your head - do not wait for them to be loud before you acknowledge them because the most ruthless predator is you.
